**Elements of the Garden**

**The Garden** (chaman) - A microcosm of paradise on earth. It may also represent the confluence of nature and man, the application of order onto the natural world, the systematic ordering of the universe.Â 

**The Nightingale** (bulbul)- the nightingale that longs for the rose, usually symbolizing the soulâ€™s desire to unify with the divine. May be captured by the hunter (sayyad) and imprisoned in a cage (qafas). Often invoked in the poetry of Mir, Ghalib, Daagh and Iqbal

**The Rose** (gul) - a symbol of the beloved. The ultimate form of beauty and perfection, both worldly (mijazi) and divine (haqiqi)Â
